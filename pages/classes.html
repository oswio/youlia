Creative Studio

Creative studio offers private art classes for children and adults. Every session is a combination of artistically stimulating activities that include drawing, painting, sculpting, paper cutting or 3D compositions. Students create projects that foster self-expression, promote discipline, encourage creativity, and nurture the artistic spirit, as well as expand their minds while having fun.
The mission of Creative Studio is to teach visual arts and explore various techniques in a safe and supportive environment, promoting experimentation and encouraging creative thinking.
